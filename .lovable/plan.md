

# EcoBloom Plant Care — Website Plan

## Overview
A stunning, Ghibli-inspired garden care website for EcoBloom Plant Care, featuring warm parchment tones, organic typography (Fraunces + Instrument Sans), and lush botanical imagery. The site will showcase services, pricing plans, and a booking form that sends details via WhatsApp.

## Sections (Single-Page)

### 1. Navigation Bar
- Logo + "EcoBloom Plant Care" branding
- Links: Home, Services, Pricing, Why Us, Contact
- "Book Now" CTA button in marigold

### 2. Hero Section
- Large headline: "Your garden, tended with heart."
- Subtitle about expert plant care starting at ₹499
- Ghibli-style garden illustration as background/overlay
- CTA: "Start your garden's journey" → scrolls to booking

### 3. Services Section ("What We Do")
- Card grid for: Monthly Care, Annual Care, Garden Design, Pest Control, Repotting, Nutrient Management, Plant Setup, Custom Green Décor
- Each card with an icon and short description from the brochure content

### 4. How It Works (3 Steps)
- Tell us about your garden → We plan the care → We take care of it
- Clean step-by-step with icons

### 5. Pricing Table — "Garden Calculator"
- **Plant count selector** (segmented control): 10, 20, 30, 40, 50, 75, 100 plants
- **Duration cards**: 1 Month, 3 Months, 6 Months, 1 Year
- Dynamic pricing display based on selection
- 1 Year card highlighted as "Best Value" with "+4 Free Visits" badge
- All prices in ₹ INR, showing monthly rate and total
- Clean table/matrix view for desktop, card stack for mobile

### 6. Why EcoBloom / What Makes Us Different
- Cards highlighting: Expert Horticulturists, Personalized Care Plans, 100% Organic Solutions, Weekly Monitoring, Healthy & Aesthetic Growth, Customized Add-Ons

### 7. Mission & Values
- Brief section with key mission points from the brochure

### 8. Testimonials
- 3 testimonial cards with customer quotes (placeholder content)

### 9. Booking Form (Multi-Step)
- **Step 1**: Service type (Maintenance / Consultation / Pest Control / Garden Design)
- **Step 2**: Garden details (plant count, address, name, phone, email)
- **Step 3**: Preferred time window selection
- On submit → Opens WhatsApp with pre-filled message containing all booking details
- Contact numbers: 9270993102 / 9322084283

### 10. Footer
- Contact info, social links (Instagram, WhatsApp)
- Email: ecobloomplantcare@gmail.com
- Service links, legal links
- "EcoBloom Plant Care — We make spaces bloom"

## Design System
- **Fonts**: Fraunces (headings), Instrument Sans (body), JetBrains Mono (pricing numbers)
- **Colors**: Warm parchment background, forest moss green primary, soft marigold CTAs
- **Cards**: 24px radius, soft shadows, no harsh borders
- **Buttons**: Rounded-full, subtle hover lift animation
- **Spacing**: Generous py-24 sections, max-w-6xl container
- **No stock photos** — emoji/SVG botanical icons for services
